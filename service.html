What We Do

Physiotherapy And Rehabilitation Treatments By Healing Hand

Physiotherapy is a wide field of science that focuses on specialized treatments aiming at restoring and enhancing physical well-being. At Healing Hand Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Center, we offer several services to help you in recovery and gaining mobility

BACK PAIN

Therapy to relieve discomfort due to back pain and restore spinal function for improved mobility

KNEE PAIN

Treatment to address joint pain and focusing on strength and flexibility of knees and enhancing them

NECK PAIN

Using techniques to ease tension, improve posture and strengthen the muscles that support the neck

SPORTS INJURIES

Therapies to speed up recovery and prevention of re-injury in athletes to promote safe participation

ANKLE PAIN

Therapies to enhance ankle flexibility, stability, improve the mobility and reduce ankle pain

BELL'S PALSY

Treatment to restore and strengthen facial muscle function, and face symmetry post Bell’s Palsy

REGULAR THERAPY

Enhance mobility, relieve pain, and improve overall well-being through Regular therapy

A C L INJURIES

Rehabilitation techniques to strengthen and stabilize the knee after a ligament injury to increase range of motion

POST OPERATIVE PHYSIOTHERAPY
