What We Do

Physiotherapy And Rehabilitation Treatments By Healing Hand

Physiotherapy is a wide field of science that focuses on specialized treatments aiming at restoring and enhancing physical well-being. At Healing Hand Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Center, we offer several services to help you in recovery and gaining mobility

SPORTS INJURIES

Therapies to speed up recovery and prevention of re-injury in athletes to promote safe participation


POST OPERATIVE PHYSIOTHERAPY

Rehabilitation plans to help in faster recovery and restoration of body functions post-surgery

GERIATRIC PHYSIOTHERAPY

Care targeting age related mobility issues that help elder people in being active and independent

FACIAL PALSY

Treatments to improve facial muscle movement, control and expressions and recovery of balanced movement

MUSCULOSKELETAL INJURIES

Treatments to address soft tissue, bone or joint injuries to promote overall physical performance and lessen discomfort

SHOULDER PAIN

Strategies focusing on shoulder strength, stability, range of motion and enhance overall shoulder function


NEURO REHABILITATION

Techniques to improve functions like muscle weakness, imbalance and impaired coordination after neurological injury

CARDIAC AND PULMONARY REHABILITATION

Therapies targeting people with cardiac or respiratory problems to improve heart and lung function
